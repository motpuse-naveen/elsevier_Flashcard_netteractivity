Dermatomes of Lower Limb: Posterior View

Comment: This illustration shows the demarcation of dermatomes according to Keegan and Garrett. While shown as distinct segments, in truth there is considerable overlap between any two adjacent dermatomes. While the dermatomes spiral around the anterior thigh and leg, on the posterior side they course in a more vertical direction.

Autonomous sensory zones mark areas of virtually pure dermatome demarcation for sensory testing clinically (shown on left calf and foot).
