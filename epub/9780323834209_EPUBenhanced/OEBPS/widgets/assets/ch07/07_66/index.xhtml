Dermatomes of Lower Limb: Anterior View

Comment: This illustration shows the demarcation of dermatomes according to Keegan and Garrett. While shown as distinct segments, in truth there is considerable overlap between any two adjacent dermatomes. The spiral configuration of the dermatomes from lateral to medial is a result of the medial rotation and torsion of the embryonic lower limb bud (big toe medial) versus the lateral rotation of the upper limb (thumb lateral).

Autonomous sensory zones mark areas of virtually pure dermatome demarcation for sensory testing clinically (shown on left lower extremity).
