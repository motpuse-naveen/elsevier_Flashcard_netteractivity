Nerves of Oral Cavity

Comment: Taste of the posterior third of the tongue is largely via the glossopharyngeal nerve while taste to the anterior two-thirds is via the facial nerve (chorda tympani). General sensation of the epiglottis is via CN X, whereas most of the posterior tongue is via CN IX, and general sensation to the anterior two-thirds is by CN V3 (lingual nerve). Some overlap is common.
